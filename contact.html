Emergency Services

Eden Contracting NY provides 24/7 emergency services for urgent sidewalk and concrete repairs. If you have a safety hazard or urgent DOT violation that needs immediate attention, don't hesitate to call us any time of day or night.

When to Call for Emergency Service:

  • Dangerous sidewalk conditions that pose safety risks
  • Urgent DOT violations with tight deadlines
  • Storm damage to concrete surfaces
  • Water main breaks affecting sidewalks
  • Any concrete emergency requiring immediate attention
